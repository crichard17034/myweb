The ruins of an ancient civilization have been discovered, and with it, all the treasure buried underneath. You have four minutes to grab what you can and get out before the cavern ceiling crashes down on top of you!

TLDR: First proper game I've ever made in Unity about treasure hunting.

Controls

  1. Arrow Keys/WASD: Walk
  2. Space Bar: Jump
  3. Shift: Sprint
